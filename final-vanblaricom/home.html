About Us

We created the Blacksburg Cat Shelter because there is a large population of stray cats in our area. We have been in the cat-loving business for over 25 years. We love all cats and we have plenty of cats that are in need of a good home. We are proud to be a no-kill shelter. We accept and encourage donations. Your donations allow us to keep our shelter running and our cats healthy! Please visit our "Available Pets" tab to see which cats we have available. If you are interested in volunteering, please contact us below.

Please donate to our shelter! You can make a difference in 20 cats lives by donating $20.
